# An Analysis of Bike-Sharing Data in New York City
## Overview
### I looked at bike-sharing data from New York City in the month of August to gain an understanding of who was using the bikes, what they were being used for, and how much demand there was overall for this service.
## Results
The initial findings are very promising.
